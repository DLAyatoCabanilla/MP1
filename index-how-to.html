Harissa Air-fried Liempo

5.0

(8)

5

1 photo

A not your ordinary Filipino fiesta dish as this was made using the technology of the air-fryer with the mix of the North African red chili paste.

Recipe by

Lloyd dee Davis

7/23/23

Rate

print

Share

INGREDIENTS

  • 7 Dry red chiles
  • 6 Roasted red peppers
  • 2 tablespoons Tomato paste
  • 4 Fresh garlic cloves
  • 1 large lemon
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cumin and Coriander
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika/Cayenne Pepper
  • 3 kgs of Pork
  • 3 tablespoons of oil

DIRECTION

Step 1:

Using a Food Processor to make the Harissa, mix the Dried Chilis, Roasted Red peppers, Tomato Paste, Garlic Cloves, Large lemon, Salt and Pepper, Cumin, Coriander and Paprika/Cayenne Pepper until the mixed spices becomes a paste.


Step 2:

Using an Air-fryer, Air-fry the 3 kgs of Pork until it becomes crispy


Step 3:

Put the mixed Harissa on the Pork until the sauce becomes mixed with the Pork


Step 4:

Serve and Enjoy!

Prep Time/Cook time

20 mins./20 mins

Total Time

40 mins

Servings

4-6

An original dish from France. It's halfway between a sauté and a stew, simmered chicken in a rich, silky, and creamy sauce.

Recipe by

Lloyd dee Davis

05/24/2023

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 whole chicken (3 ½ to 4 pounds), cut into 11 pieces (reserve back and wing tips for another use, such as stock)
  • 1 large can of mushrooms (400g)
  • 2 boxes of all-purpose cream (250g)
  • 2 spoons of Butter
  • Parsley
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

DIRECTION

Step 1:

Put oil and Pan-fry the chicken 10 to 15 mins and set aside.


Step 2:

Saute the onions and garlic in 2 mins. Afterwards, open the can of mushrooms and pour it on the pan with the sauteed onions and garlic.


Step 3:

Add the 2 boxes of all-purpose cream and bring it to a boil until the cream is thick to bring out the flavorful sauce.


Step 4:

Add the pan-fried chicken with butter and turn off the heat if the creamy sauce is thick. Add Parsley as Garnish.

Currywurst

4.7

(15)

Known as Germany's most-iconic street food, This dish features the bite-sized hotdog/bradwurst with curry-flavored ketchup.

Recipe by

Dan Lloyd Cabanilla

05/25/2023

Rate

print

Share

INGREDIENTS

  • Defrosted & Uncooked Hotdogs/1 pound uncooked bradwurst
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 2 cups ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups of water

DIRECTION

Step 1:

Place hotdogs/bratwurst and water in a skillet; cover and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes. Uncover; turn bratwurst and cook until browned and centers are no longer pink, 13-17 minutes longer. Remove and cool slightly. Cut into 1/2-in.-thick slices.


Step 2:

In the same skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion; cook and stir until tender, 5-7 minutes. Add ketchup and curry powder. Bring to a boil; reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, until slightly thickened, 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add sliced hotdog/bratwurst; heat through.


Step 3:

Sprinkle with additional curry powder and serve it onto a plate.

Prep Time/Cook Time

15 minutes/40 minutes

Total Time

55 minutes

Servings

This pad kra pao (pork & holy basil-stir-fry) features a key ingredient: holy basil! With jasmine rice, it’s perfection. And it only takes minutes to make.

Recipe by

Lloyd dee Davis

03/01/2023

Rate

print

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • Onions (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2 whole red-chilis
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 pound of ground pork
  • 2 Holy Basil leaves
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce

DIRECTION

Step 1:

In a wok over medium high heat, add the oil, shallots and garlic, and fry for 3 minutes. Add the chilies and cook for another minute.


Step 2:

Crank up the heat to high, and add the ground pork, breaking it up into small bits and allowing it to crisp up.


Step 3:

Add the sugar, fish sauce, thin/light soy sauce, ddark soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Stir-fry for another minute and deglaze the pan with the broth or water. Because your pan is over high heat, the liquid should cook off very quickly. Add the holy basil leaves, and stir-fry until wilted.


Step 4:

Serve this Pork Pad Kra Pao(Holy Basil Stir-fry) over rice!

Crispy Tofu Sisig is a dish composed of deep fried tofu cubes, chopped chili peppers, and onion. It is healthier alternative to traditional pork sisig because it does not use any meat and greasy ingredients.

Dan Lloyd Cabanilla

INGREDIENTS

  • 14 oz. extra firm tofu sliced into small cubes
  • 2 long green pepper chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups cooking oil
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of oil

DIRECTION

Step 1:

Heat oil in a cooking pot and Once the oil gets hot, deep-fry the cubed tofu until it turns crispy. Remove from the pot using a kitchen strainer. Put in a metal colander or into a plate lined with paper towel. Set aside.


Step 2:

Scoop 3 tablespoons of oil from the pot where the tofu were fried. Pour it in a clean pan. Heat the pan on a stove top. Saute onion, long green peppers, and chili..


Step 3:

Add the crispy fried tofu. Stir-fry for 1 minute.


Step 4:

Serve it with a dipping sauce composed of soy sauce and crushed chili powders. Share and enjoy!

Seafood Paella

5.0

(10)

5

REVIEWS

1 photo

This chicken-stocked recipe is loaded with mussels, clams, peas, pan-fried chunks and shrimp.

Recipe by

Lloyd dee Davis & Dan Lloyd Cabanilla

06/23/2023

Rate

print

Share

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 whole tomato (finely chopped)
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 1 can of Green peas
  • Turmeric Powder
  • 1 Del Monte Tomato Paste
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cups of rice
  • 1 pound large (21-24 per pound) shrimp, peeled and deveined, with tails left on
  • 1 pound mussels, rinsed and scrubbed
  • 1 pound littleneck clams, rinsed and scrubbed
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 4 1/2 cups chicken stock

DIRECTION

Step 1:

In a saucepan over medium heat, bring the chicken stock to a boil. Turn off the heat and let the chicken stock for 15 minutes


Step 2:

Pan-fry the shrimp or any other chunks that you will use for your paella. (Ex. Chunks are Sliced Chicken, Meat, etc.)


Step 3:

In a large deep pan or cast iron skillet, heat 3 tablespoon olive oil. Turn the heat to medium-high and add the chopped onions and garlic Saute the garlic & onions for 2 minutes, then add the Del Monte Tomato Paste first then add the rice, and cook for 3 more minutes, stirring regularly. Now add the turmeric powder and the chicken stock. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and green beans. Bring to a boil and let the liquid slightly reduce, then cover (with lid or tightly with foil) and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.


Step 4:

Uncover and spread the pan-fried shrimp over the rice, pushing it into the rice slightly. Add a little water if needed. Add the mussels & clams and Cover and cook for another 10 minutes or until the shrimp turns pink. Finally, add the cooked chunks and the finely chopped bell peppers that you will use for your paella. When the paella is warmed through, turn heat off and serve the paella hot. Enjoy and appreciate what you made.

Prep Time/Cook Time

30 minutes/35 minutes

Total Time

65 minutes

Servings

6
