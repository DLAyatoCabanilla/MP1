This chicken-stocked recipe is loaded with mussels, clams, peas,chicken and shrimp.

Recipe by

Lloyd dee Davis & Dan Lloyd Cabanilla

06/23/2023

Rate

print

Share

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 whole tomato (finely chopped)
  • ½ onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 1 can of Green peas
  • Turmeric Powder
  • 1 Del Monte Tomato Paste
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cups of rice
  • 1 pound large (21-24 per pound) shrimp, peeled and deveined, with tails left on
  • 1 pound mussels, rinsed and scrubbed
  • 1 pound littleneck clams, rinsed and scrubbed
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 4 1/2 cups chicken stock

DIRECTION

Step 1:

In a large deep pan or cast iron skillet, heat 3 tablespoon olive oil. Turn the heat to medium-high and add the chopped onions, garlic and tomato. Saute the garlic & onions for 2 minutes then add the rice, and cook for 3 more minutes, stirring regularly. Now add the chopped garlic and the chicken stock. Stir in the saffron and it's soaking liquid, paprika, cayenne pepper, aleppo pepper, and salt. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and green beans. Bring to a boil and let the liquid slightly reduce, then cover (with lid or tightly with foil) and cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
