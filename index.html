RECIPES

BEEF SALPICAO

Beef Salpicao is a beef stir-fry dish flavored with Worcestershire, butter, garlic, and red chili pepper flakes. It's quick and simple to make yet packs fantastic flavor. Delicious as an appetizer or main dish! This said dish was made by Filipinos influenced by Spanish culture.

favorite 2.5k

CHICKEN FRICASSEE

Chicken fricassee has been described as "a standard old-fashioned American dish." It was one of Abraham Lincoln's favorite dishes. It's the typical "comfort food" of Cajun cuisine. The chicken is browned, then slowly braised with onions, garlic, herbs and carrots in a white wine cream sauce.

favorite 2.5k

CURRYWURST

Originated in Germany, Currywurst is known as Germany's most iconic street food. It eventually spread to other cities and different variations were made. Key features of this classy-iconic street food are the sliced-bite sized hotdog/bradwurst smothered with curry flavored tomato ketchup, sprinkled with mustard/paprika/curry powder.

favorite 100.2k

LEMON-BUTTERED LIEMPO

Liempo has been a favorite food of the Filipinos. Mixing it with Fresh-squeezed Lemon Juice with melted butter on a pan and the result is super juicy and flavorful Liempo with the perfect combination of sour, sweet and acidic notes.

favorite 2.5k

PAN-FRIED TUNA

Pan-fried tuna are served medium-rare and taste fancy but are very easy to cook. A sprinkling of cracked black pepper is a good finisher for this dish. This makes for a quick and delicious dinner after a long day with almost no effort.

favorite 2.5k

TOFU SISIG

Sisig refers to a finely chopped pork dish of Filipino origin. But instead of using pork, we are going to use tofu for a more healthy option. It can be consumed as an appetizer and a main dish.Season it with a dash of calamansi, onions and chili peppers for a more complex taste.

favorite 2.5k

SEAFOOD PAELLA

This saffron-infused paella recipe is loaded with mussels, clams, and shrimp. The origins of paella are ancient, rooted in the area around Valencia, Spain near the Albufera Lagoon, where farm workers of their own compliment each other's dish by what they find.

favorite 2.5k

PAD KRA PAO

Thai basil chicken, better known in Thai as pad kra pao is a contender for the most popular, and the most beloved Thai street food dish of all time. However, this dish will require the Authentic Holy Basil which is the key feature of this dish. Luckily, at home we can easily grow these plants and cook it right at your homes.

favorite 2.5k

HARISSA AIR-FRIED LIEMPO

This classic Filipino Dish is not your ordinary food as this requires an Air Fryer. Adding the culture of North Africa by putting "Harissa" onto the dish adds a spicy kick and an addictive complex taste to the tongue.
